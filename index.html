Our Story

Where craftsmanship meets artistry

A Legacy of Excellence

Since 1987, Luminère has been crafting exceptional jewelry that transcends time. Each piece tells a story, celebrates a moment, and becomes part of your personal heritage.

Our master artisans blend traditional techniques with contemporary design, ensuring every creation is both timeless and unique. We source only the finest materials, committed to ethical practices and sustainable luxury.

From engagement rings to statement necklaces, our collections are designed to make you feel extraordinary, because we believe jewelry is more than adornment—it's an expression of who you are.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Luminère, we believe luxury and responsibility go hand in hand. We're committed to creating beautiful jewelry while protecting the planet and supporting communities.

  • Ethical Sourcing: All our diamonds are conflict-free and certified by the Kimberley Process
  • Recycled Metals: We use 100% recycled gold and platinum in our designs
  • Fair Labor: Our partners adhere to strict fair labor practices
  • Community Support: We invest 5% of profits into mining community education programs
  • Carbon Neutral: Our operations are fully carbon neutral by 2025

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom design services?

Yes! Our master jewelers work closely with you to create bespoke pieces that reflect your unique vision. From initial sketch to final polish, we guide you through every step of the custom design process.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all purchases, excluding custom pieces. Items must be in original condition with all documentation. Engagement rings come with a 60-day return window.

Do you provide jewelry appraisals?

Yes, we offer professional appraisal services for both insurance purposes and resale value. Our certified gemologists provide detailed documentation of your pieces.

How do I care for my jewelry?

Each piece comes with specific care instructions. Generally, we recommend regular cleaning, safe storage, and annual professional inspections. We offer complimentary cleaning services for all our pieces.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we partner with select financing companies to offer flexible payment plans. Options include 0% interest for qualified buyers. Contact us for more details.

Are your diamonds conflict-free?

Absolutely. All our diamonds are certified conflict-free and comply with the Kimberley Process. We are committed to ethical sourcing and sustainability across all our materials.
